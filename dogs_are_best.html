Dogs truly are a man's best friend.

For as long as anyone can remember they've heard the familiar saying that "Dogs are a man's best friend." That's because it's always been true and will remain to be true until the end of time. Dogs are the most loyal creature on the face of the planet Earth, and will always be right by your side, tail wagging and eyes glimmering. They treat their humans like a King or Queen, which rules their world. If you're lacking attention and admiraction in your life, a dog will be sure to fill that void and meet your needs and wants in a best friend, time and time again.



Some things to enjoy with your dog:

  • Long walks on the beach, or anywhere, really
  • Playing fetch at the drop of a hat *cough* ball
  • Teaching them new tricks
  • Showing off said tricks to impress your friends, family, or that crush you've been wanting to break the ice with
  • Your dog will protect you at all costs
  • They will be there simply by calling their name


A little more about why dogs are the truly the best animal...

If you put the time and energy into training and spending time with your dog, you will receive everything you put into your best friend right back in the form of a loyal companion that will follow your lead. A dog can be trained to do so many things - the possibilities are endless - all the while significantly increasing your happiness while feeling a sense of accomplishment for the work you put in. Owning a dog truly is a win-win! Dogs have amazing personalities and can also keep you entertained for hours. They can be absolutely hilarious at times and do things to make you laugh constantly.
